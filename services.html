Services

Primary Care

We provide general medical services such as vaccinations, basic health screenings, and treatment for minor illnesses and injuries. This could include services like physical examinations, blood pressure checks, diabetes screenings, and basic laboratory testing.

Telehealth Services

With the advancement of technology, a mobile clinic could offer telehealth services, allowing patients to receive medical consultations, follow-ups, and prescriptions remotely through video or phone calls. This could be particularly useful for patients in remote or underserved areas who may not have easy access to medical facilities.

Mental Health Services

Mobile clinics could offer mental health services, such as counseling, therapy, and mental health assessments. This could be particularly beneficial for individuals in remote areas or communities with limited access to mental health resources.
