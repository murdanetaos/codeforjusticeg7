Services

Primary Care

We provide general medical services such as vaccinations, basic health screenings, and treatment for minor illnesses and injuries. This could include services like physical examinations, blood pressure checks, diabetes screenings, and basic laboratory testing.

Telehealth Services

With the advancement of technology, We can offer telehealth services, allowing patients to receive medical consultations, follow-ups, and prescriptions remotely through video or phone calls. This could be particularly useful for patients in remote or underserved areas who may not have easy access to medical facilities.

Women's health services

We also provide women's health services, such as reproductive health screenings, family planning services, prenatal care, and basic gynecological care. These services can help improve women's health outcomes and access to essential healthcare.
